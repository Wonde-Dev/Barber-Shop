The Blade & Co. Experience

Founded in 2016, Blade & Co. is not just a barbershop — it's a sanctuary for modern gentlemen who appreciate precision, tradition, and creativity. Our master barbers combine classic techniques with contemporary styles, ensuring every client walks out with a fresh, tailored look.

We use premium organic products, straight razor finishes, and hot lather for the ultimate grooming experience. Our space breathes vintage energy blended with urban luxury — exposed brick, leather chairs, and the sound of clippers like a symphony.
